Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

General

How long does a typical cabinet project take?

Most kitchen projects take 4-6 weeks from design approval to delivery. Bathroom vanities typically take 2-3 weeks. Complex custom projects may take longer depending on specifications.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer free in-home consultations and measurements. Our designers will visit your space, discuss your needs, and provide a detailed quote at no obligation.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire New Jersey area and surrounding states. Contact us to confirm if we service your location.

Design & Customization

Can I customize my cabinet design?

Absolutely! That's our specialty. We can customize sizes, colors, materials, hardware, and finishes to match your exact specifications and style preferences.

Do you provide 3D design renderings?

Yes, we provide detailed 3D renderings so you can visualize your new cabinets before delivery. This helps ensure you're completely satisfied with the design.

What cabinet styles do you offer?

We offer a wide range of styles including Shaker, Modern, Traditional, Transitional, and Custom designs. We also offer various wood species and finishes.

Pricing & Payment

What is the average cost of kitchen cabinets?

Prices vary based on size, materials, and customization. Our kitchen cabinets typically range from $8,000 to $50,000+. We offer options for various budgets.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, check, and major credit cards.

Delivery & Service

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We provide fast delivery within 7 business days or less.

What warranty do you offer?

We provide a 5-year warranty on cabinet construction and a 5-year warranty on hardware. Additional warranties are available.

What if I have issues after delivery?

We stand behind our work. Contact us anytime and we'll address any concerns promptly. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help. Contact us and we'll get back to you promptly.
